Infrastructure changes are Git-tracked, operationally validated, and governed through reproducible workflows.

Platform engineering decisions prioritize operational efficiency and stability under constrained hardware conditions.

Observability-First Operations

Metrics, logs, audits, and operational visibility are treated as first-class platform requirements.

Recovery-Centric Engineering

Backup validation, recovery testing, and incident documentation are integrated into operational workflows.
